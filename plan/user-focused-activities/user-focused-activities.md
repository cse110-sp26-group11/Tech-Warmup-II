# Personas

## Persona 1: Casual Player

**Name:** Billy Bob Joe III     
**Age:** 30     
**Job:** Stock Broker       
**Device:** PC      
**Likes:** Short, frequent sessions         

### About Billy Bob Joe III
Billy plays slots when he has free time. This could be during breaks at work, after he comes home from work, or when he's riding on the bus to and from work. Billy doesn't care about strategies on how to win big, he just wants a fun game to play. 

### Goals
- Make an enjoyable game people can come back to
- Have frequent small wins to keep the player engaged
- Implement colors and effects to make the game appealing

### Likes
- Billy likes to log on to the game to collect his daily rewards
- He likes games with free spins, bonuses, and showy animations
- Billy doesn't like to think too hard about spinning, so he frequently uses the auto spin feature
- Notifications grab Billy's attention, he always makes sure to participate in limited time events. 

### Dislikes
- Billy hates going on losing streaks
- He doesn't have a full understanding of odds or payouts
- However, he still doesn't like when the payout is less than his wager.

### Motivations
- When the wheel just barely ticks over what he wants, Billy gets more engaged.
- Billy enjoys the flashing lights and satisfying bells.
- Daily log in streaks keep Billy in the habit of playing

## Persona 2: Wants to Make Money

**Name:** Chuck Hutcherson      
**Age:** 28     
**Job:** In between jobs        
**Device** Home PC      
**Likes:** Long play sessions with huge bets        

### Background
Chuck passed his stats class and understands probabilites, and he has made peace with the fact that the house always wins. However, he still loves to optimize his strategies and bet big. 

### Goals
- Getting the maximum amount of money from each bet

### Likes
- Games with high payouts, even if the loss could be big
- Getting as many multipliers and bonuses as possible
- To strategically adjust how much he bets
- Playing fewer, longer sessions with big bets.

### Dislikes
- Not knowing possible payouts beforehand
- Not clearly understanding how the odds work
- Overly gamified machines, he thinks they are manipulative.

### Motivations
- The dopamine boost of finally hitting a huge payout
- Having a sense of control, being able to stop whenever he wants

# User Stories

## Gameplay

### 1. Spinning
As a casual player, I want to quickly spin and see results with exciting animations so that I feel entertained even in short sessions.

### 2. Winning Feedback
As a casual player, I want to feel rewarded when I win. This could be due to exciting visuals and sounds cues to keep me motivated and stay playing.

---

## Features

### 3. Bonus Features 
As a frequent casual player, I want features like bonus spins, special events, and wild slots to keep the game fresh. 

### 4. Control & Interaction
As a casual player, I want to be able to use the auto spin feature to avoid thinking too hard, but I also want to control when I stop. 

---

## Retention

### 5. Daily Rewards System
As a frequent player, I want daily login rewards and streaks so I am motivated to come back an play every day

---

## Advanced Players

### 6. Transparency
As a strategic advanced player, I want access to the odds and knowledge of payouts as well as volatility, so I can make informed decisions about what games to play.

### 7. Events
As a frequent advanced player, I want special events and limited time missions so I feel a sense of urgency and progression.
