# User Personas & Stories

## Persona 1: Marie the Mom
* [cite_start]**Demographics:** 35 years old, middle class[cite: 1, 2]. [cite_start]Stay-at-home mom with two elementary-school-aged sons[cite: 2, 3].
* [cite_start]**Motivations:** Needs to pass mundane downtime alone with something fun on her phone to destress (e.g., waiting for kids in the car or at the DMV)[cite: 5, 6]. 
* [cite_start]**Behavior:** Not concerned about winning big; just wants quick dopamine while bored[cite: 7].
* **Key User Stories:**
    * [cite_start]Wants to play a casual free game to pass the time[cite: 30].

## Persona 2: Gary the Gambler
* [cite_start]**Demographics:** 68-year-old bachelor, recently retired corporate manager[cite: 8, 9]. [cite_start]Spends time at his vacation house boating and drinking with retired friends[cite: 10, 11].
* [cite_start]**Motivations:** Former Vegas regular who loves the high-stakes adrenaline rush of gambling but no longer wants to travel[cite: 12, 13, 15]. 
* [cite_start]**Behavior:** Wants to utilize his statistical casino knowledge, but **cannot** risk his life savings[cite: 35, 47].
* **Key User Stories:**
    * [cite_start]Wants to feel the rush of gambling from home, necessitating authentic win celebrations and satisfying feedback[cite: 25, 27, 28].
    * [cite_start]Needs clear UI stating no real-life money is required, utilizing a closed-loop in-game currency with ways to regain it if he runs out[cite: 37, 38, 39].
    * [cite_start]Desires underlying probability/winnings calculations that allow for player skill/strategy, rather than just pure luck[cite: 49].
